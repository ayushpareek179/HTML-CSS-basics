About the language

The cup says Beautiful Life in Castellano, another name for Spanish

Spanish is one of the many Romance languages, that originated in Iberia. It was transported to Americas, Western Africa and East Indies by imperialistic conquistadores (conquerors) and has since become the first language of over 500 million people globally.

It has various dialects and marked changes in pronunciations in vocabulary across geographies. Countries where Spanish is an official/national/de facto language, in order of population:
